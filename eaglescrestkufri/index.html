Our Story

A Home in the Clouds

Where family warmth meets Himalayan grandeur

A Family's Dream, A Traveler's Paradise

Nestled in the serene hills of Kufri, Himachal Pradesh, Eagles Crest Homestay is more than just a place to stay — it is the realization of a family's dream to share the untouched beauty of the Himalayas with the world.

Built and lovingly run by our family, every corner of this home reflects our passion for hospitality and our deep connection to these mountains. From the handpicked furnishings to the home-cooked Himachali meals, every detail is crafted to make you feel like family.हिमाचल की पहाड़ियों में बसा यह होमस्टे हमारे परिवार का सपना है। हर कोने में पहाड़ों से मोहब्बत और मेहमाननवाज़ी की गर्माहट मिलेगी।

Wake up to mist-kissed valleys, spend your afternoons exploring hidden trails, and end your evenings around a crackling bonfire under a canopy of stars. At Eagles Crest, time slows down, and the mountains whisper their ancient secrets.
